Manage Disibility Confident employers prototype

This is for testing only, do not use it if you want something real to happen.

Version 1 - shown to agents by Business Analyst and Product Support as an initial prototype

Interactions added:

  • find a record
  • view a record
  • accept a registration
  • change Disability Confident level, see level 1 commitments
  • delete a record

Start - version 1

Version 2 - used with agents and team leaders in user research

Interactions added or updated:

  • sign in, as only certain agents will be able to do the work
  • view the list of registered employers
  • see how many items are listed, as the team need to monitor volumes
  • see what status items in the list have (eg through status tags)
  • send a confirmation email when an employer is accepted containing the information they need, eg through integration with Notify
  • send a confirmation email when an employer is rejected containing the information they need, eg through integration with Notify
  • indicate user can export employer records, as DC employers are reported on and published on GOV.UK
  • show the approved date and expiry date (which will be 3 years after their approved date) for an approved company
  • add prototype banner for user research purposes
  • reject an employer registration
  • organise the items in the list (there'll be over 10,000), eg through pagination
  • show record of actions taken against a record by agents, eg through the timeline pattern - which seems like it should sit under in the 'Case actions' section
  • add way of changing other company details, eg contact name, address
  • update employer approved page so it reflects they are emailed automatically
  • remove 'action needed' - was told that agents wish to handle this through Fresh Desk
  • fix routing that's already been added for Disability Confident levels and the commitment pages these take the user to - something is stopping this taking effect

Start - version 2

Version 3

Interactions added or updated:

  • email at the start of the prototype indicates how agents will be alerted to new registrations
  • list of companies includes company name, postcode, contact name, level, status, ID
  • no ID in the list for new registrations, as they've not been accepted yet
  • add 'change' links by details in company record
  • add alert message above a new record to remind agent to check for duplicates, while they're in the context of looking at their details
  • change 'confirm status' button to 'approve registration', as purpose was previously unclear
  • add question about checking for duplicates as part of approval flow, as this is a check agents have to make
  • add guidance page for where agent answered that the company is a duplicate - actions they need to take next
  • add pages for agents to enter the company details above and beyond what they receive from the company as part of the 'approve' interaction before confirmation - selecting the matching jobcentre district after consulting FIND (the group matching the selection automatically), selecting the type of public sector employer they are if applicable
  • add 'reject company' link to list of company actions, as the way to do this was previously unclear

Interactions to add later:

  • indicate change of details as they are made in the rest of the prototype
  • how we present the list of records (there are also examples in the DWP design patterns backlog) - I've added a table for now
  • find or concentrate on certain records, such as those that are pending, through faceted search or by sorting the items
  • resend certificate badge if company has lost their material
  • example of a record for an expired employer, as a DC certificate can expire without them renewing it or asking to be removed from the scheme
